The Cauldron is a circular inland sea, wrapped by [Dylund]() and the [Sheepstrath]() to the west and north, and by the [Ingle]() and [Halfisle]() to the east and south. It has lesser [tides](the-tide) than most shores of the open sea, and there is no need, there, for summerports and winterports.
